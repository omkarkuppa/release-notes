  Avoid saving volatile DDRIO register data to NVRAM.
  
  [Feature Description]
  In order to avoid NVRAM reclaim operations, NVRAM variables must not change from boot to boot.
  MRC must avoid caching all of the fields in DDRCOMP_RX_RETRAIN0 since some will be updated by HW at runtime. This
                                                                                                ^^                  
  results in the cache variable containin DDRCOMP_RX_RETRAIN0 changing from boot to boot and driving NVRAM reclaim
  operations.
  
  [Resolution]
  Cache only the fields in DDRCOMP_RX_RETRAIN0 that are required for fast boot and are known to not change from boot to boot.
