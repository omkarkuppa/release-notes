[ME]

  GetRuleData PEI uninitialized buffer fix
  
  [Issue Description]
  RuleData is defined in BIOS as 4 bytes, but it's not always
  filled with data. CSME FW can respond with just 1 byte of data,
  and the rest of the buffer is not touched.
  
  Example is UserConsent flow, where BIOS should expect just
  1 byte, but because it wasn't cleared before, it could contain
  some garbage data and result in skipping UserConsent flow.
  
  [Resolution]
  Whole message buffer should be zeroed before using.
  
  Package/Module:
  OneSiliconPkg
