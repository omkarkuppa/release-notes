  BIOS WA for MTL-S/ARL-S RVP pad broken issue
  
  [Feature Description]

  IMVP MOSFETs by programming corresponding registers via SMBUS:
  a. SMBUS device address: 0x20
  b. Set BIT4 to 1 to register 0x9F/0xB5/0xC5
